Planning and Preparation

It's important to plan your trip ahead of time and do some research on the places you want to visit. This will help you get a better idea of what to expect and make your trip smoother.

Accommodation

India offers a wide range of accommodation options, including budget hostels, mid-range hotels, and luxury resorts. It's important to book your accommodation in advance, especially during peak tourist season.

Sightseeing

India is known for its rich history and cultural heritage. Some must-visit places include the Taj Mahal, Jaipur, Udaipur, Delhi, Mumbai, and Goa. However, there are many other hidden gems that are worth exploring.
